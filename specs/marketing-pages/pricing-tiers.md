# Pricing Tier Definitions

## Tier 1: Starter

**Target Audience:** Small teams just getting started with structured onboarding

**Features:**
- Up to 10 active users
- 5 onboarding plans per month
- Basic onboarding templates
- Task checklists and timelines
- Team directory
- Email support
- Standard branding

## Tier 2: Professional

**Target Audience:** Growing companies scaling their onboarding process

**Features:**
- Up to 50 active users
- Unlimited onboarding plans
- Advanced onboarding templates
- Custom onboarding workflows
- Task checklists and timelines
- Team directory with buddy assignments
- Weekly reflection forms
- AI-powered chat assistant
- Analytics dashboard
- Priority email support
- Custom branding options
- API access

## Tier 3: Enterprise

**Target Audience:** Large organizations with complex onboarding needs

**Features:**
- Unlimited active users
- Unlimited onboarding plans
- Advanced onboarding templates
- Custom onboarding workflows
- Task checklists and timelines
- Team directory with role-based assignments
- Weekly reflection forms
- AI-powered chat assistant with custom training
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Manager effectiveness tracking
- Retention trend analysis
- Dedicated account manager
- Phone & email support
- White-label branding
- SSO integration
- Advanced API access
- Custom integrations
- 99.9% SLA uptime guarantee
- Onboarding consultation services
